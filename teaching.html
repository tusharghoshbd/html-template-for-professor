I'm the Director of Postgraduate Taught (PGT) Programmes within the Cardiff School of Mathematics.

  • MSc in Operational Research and Applied Statistics
  • MSc in Operational Research, Applied Statistics and Financial Risk
  • MSc in Data Science and Analytics

More information on these programmes an be found here.


It's been a privileged opportunity (if not somewhat hard work!) to have designed and established these MSc programmes, in conjunction with colleagues and external organisations (especially Office for National Statistics and Welsh Government ). The hard work of all the teaching and admin staff involved with these programmes, and the innovative content and structure, was recognised through the award of the Cardiff University 2011 Innovation Prize.
